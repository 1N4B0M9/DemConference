Become an Ambassador!

One person from each school in attendance will be chosen to be an ambassador for PA YDC. The role of the ambassador is to communicate details of the conference to the students attending from their school and recruit more students from their school to attend the conference. This role is a great opportunity to aim leadership experience and connect with democrats in your school community.
